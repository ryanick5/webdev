Violins

Violins are the smallest of the strings instruments, and are able to play the highest pitch. They are often given the melody.

Violas

Violas are slightly larger than the violins, but are played with the instrument sitting on the shoulder of the musician, just like violins. They often play the inner voices, and are the most hated stringed instrument.

Cellos

Cellos are played with the instrument stood upright on an endpin. They can be given the baseline, inner voices, but are known for beatiful higher melodies as well.

Basses

Basses almost exclusively do as their name suggests, and play the bassline. They were originally made to play the cello part, but in a lower octave. Can be used in jazz when plucked.
