Sit on the edge of your chair, with your back arched and your head up. Your wrist should be straight, and your violin should be positioned on your should angled slightly to the left. Rest your chin/jaw on your chinrest.

For your bowhold, you should have the tip of your pinky resting on top of the frog of the bow. You have your next teo fingers draped over the bow, and your pointer separated from the other fingers. Your thumb should be curved and under the wood of the bow. To bow, press the frog of your bow right after the bridge, and pull across the string.

Your fingers should be right at the beginning of the finger board, and arched over the string. To make a note, pick a string to bow on, and press one of your fingers onto the string you are bowwing. On any string, you can play open/no finger, low/normal first finger, low/high second finger, normal/high third, and low/normal fourth finger.
